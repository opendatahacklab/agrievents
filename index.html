INTRODUCTION

The agrievents project aims to provide tools for agrifood farmers and market organizers to publish as open data information about markets where agrifood products ban be bought. A technical report describing how market events and products are described is provided, along with a sample application. This sample application relies on a rdf-store accessed via a SPARQL Endpoint.
